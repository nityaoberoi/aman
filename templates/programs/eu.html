The Bulgarian immigrant-investor program offers one of the fastest tracks to a European citizenship. Basic participation in the program requires to make an investment of 1,000,000 BGN (€ 511,292) in a guaranteed government bond portfolio for a period of five years, with zero accrued interest. An alternative exists to pay a reduced amount of €180,000, which however would not be returned to the investor.

With either option, the investor receives permanent residency status within four to six months after submitting his/her application. Physical residency requirements can be waived so that the Investor can travel in and out of Bulgaria and the European Union without any restrictions.

In addition, Bulgaria offers now a fast-track to citizenship, which can be applied for as early as one year after receiving permanent residency status. For this, the Investor would have to make a second investment of €512,000 in a Bulgarian company carrying out a Priority Investment Project.
